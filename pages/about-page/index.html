About

PHDS Designs is a showcase of Paricce Hermione Delos Santos' passion for web design and development, where creativity meets technical expertise. This website shares Paricce's knowledge, latest projects, and connects with like-minded individuals. Visitors can expect to find curated web design projects, tutorials, guides, and resources, as well as insights into Paricce's design process and industry trends. With a focus on user experience, typography, and color theory, PHDS Designs inspires and delights those who appreciate well-crafted websites.
